📝 Meeting Minutes

1. Current Bestsellers Review

Team reviewed sales data from March 2026. The top three items remain:

  1. Rose Croissant — 1,240 units sold
  2. Chili Oil Sourdough Loaf — 980 units sold
  3. Black Sesame Roll — 870 units sold

The team agreed to keep these as core menu staples.
However, feedback suggests customers want a larger size for the cinnamon roll.

2. New Seasonal Pastry Ideas

We brainstormed summer flavors. Ideas include:

  • Orange Citrus Scone
  • Hawthorne Poppyseed Muffin
  • Blueberry Galette with basil herb
  • Hojicha White Chocolate Croissant

Chef Maria suggested adding a vegan (tofu) chocolate tart — the group loved this idea.

3. Customer Feedback Discussion

We reviewed comments from the suggestion box and online reviews.
Key takeaways:

  • More standard flavor options requested.
  • Customers want breakfast sandwiches available until 12 PM.
  • Several requests for lower sugar muffins.

Decision: Test chocolate muffin recipes next week with monkfruit.
